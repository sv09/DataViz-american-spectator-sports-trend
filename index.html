Change in America's favorite spectator sport

Football has been the most popular spectator sport for Americans for over a decade now, but, Soccer has been soaring in popularity lately. Interestingly, the number of people who don't watch any sport is also rising.

Source: data.world (MakeOverMonday 2020 week1) | Original data from Gallup

Note: Data between years 2008 and 2013 and between 2013 and 2017 are missing
